Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Invisible, self-healing protection for bumpers, sills and leading edges. Ideal for new cars or high-mileage vehicles.

What PPF does

  • Protects against stone chips and abrasion
  • Self-healing topcoat on many modern films
  • Can be applied to full car or high-impact zones
