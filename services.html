Our Detailing Packages

We offer a selection of carefully designed packages to suit your vehicle's needs and your budget. From essential maintenance to a complete showroom restoration, every service is performed with meticulous care and professional-grade products.

GOLD TIER: Enhancement Detail

For the discerning owner looking to restore that showroom shine. This package bridges the gap between a valet and full paint correction, delivering a significant visual transformation.

From £220
  • Single-Stage Machine Polish: Restores deep gloss and clarity while removing light swirl marks and paint defects.
  • Premium Paint Protection: Finished with a premium paint sealant or an entry-level ceramic coating for durable protection.
  • Full Chemical Decontamination: Removes embedded iron and tar that washing alone cannot, ensuring a perfectly smooth surface.
  • Hygienic Interior Deep Clean: Features steam sanitation to kill germs on all hard surfaces and hot water extraction to remove deep-seated dirt from carpets and seats.
  • Fabric or Leather Shield: Interior surfaces are treated with a protective coating to guard against future spills and stains.
  • Includes all services from the Silver Package.

SILVER TIER: The Full Valet (Most Popular)

Our reputation is built on this service. It's a comprehensive reset for your vehicle, delivering photo-worthy results and long-lasting protection inside and out.

From £100
  • Hygienic Interior Deep Clean: We use professional-grade steam to sanitize plastics and air vents, followed by hot water shampoo and extraction on seats and carpets to remove ingrained dirt, stains, and odours.
  • Full Chemical Decontamination: A critical step to remove bonded iron and tar contaminants for a truly clean and smooth paint finish.
  • Durable Paint Protection: A high-quality hand wax or sealant is applied, providing up to 6 months of protection and deep shine.
  • Complete Exterior Safe Wash: Includes a two-bucket safe wash, wheel and arch cleaning, and dressing for tyres and exterior plastics.
  • A thorough vacuum, interior wipe-down, and glass cleaning.

BRONZE TIER: Maintenance Valet

The ideal service for vehicles in good condition that need a professional touch. Perfect for regular upkeep to maintain that pristine look and feel.

From £50
  • Professional Exterior Safe Wash: Utilises a pre-wash, snow foam, and the two-bucket method to prevent scratches and maintain your paint's finish.
  • Wheels, Arches & Door Shuts: These often-missed areas are meticulously cleaned.
  • Short-Term Protection: Finished with a high-gloss spray sealant to add shine and a layer of protection.
  • Thorough Interior Tidy: A comprehensive vacuum of carpets, seats, and the boot.
  • Cabin Refresh: Dashboard, plastics, and interior glass are all carefully cleaned and wiped down.
