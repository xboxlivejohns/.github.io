Our correction process

Machine polishing is about precision. We inspect every panel, identify defect types, and build a correction plan that balances cutting power with clear-coat preservation.

  • Assessment: Lighting checks, paint depth readings, and test spot to dial in pad and polish combinations.
  • Correction: Targeted compounding to remove swirls, oxidation, and isolated scratches without compromising clear coat health.
  • Refinement: Finishing polish to create mirror reflections, followed by protection with wax, sealant, or ceramic coating.
