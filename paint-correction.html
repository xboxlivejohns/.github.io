Paint Correction — restore clarity and gloss

Multi-stage machine polishing removes swirl marks, light scratches and oxidation. We assess paint depth and recommend a safe correction plan.

What we do

  • Condition assessment & paint thickness measurement
  • Decontamination (iron fallout & tar removal)
  • Multi-stage machine polishing and finishing
  • Final protection (sealant or ceramic top coat)

Typical times: 2–8 hours depending on stage count and vehicle size.

Pricing guide

From £150 (single-stage small cars) to £450+ for premium multi-stage correction.
