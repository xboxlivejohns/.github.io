What is paint correction?

Paint correction levels the clear coat microscopically to remove or reduce surface defects. We work with measured, safe techniques — not aggressive “cut & buff”. The result is a truer, deeper gloss that lasts longer when protected.

Common defects

  • Swirl marks from poor washing
  • Light scratches & marring
  • Oxidation / faded paint
  • Water spots & haze

Our approach

  • Paint depth readings (safety first)
  • Pad & polish system matched to paint
  • Panel-by-panel correction & inspection
  • Finishing polish for maximum clarity

Why it lasts

  • True defect removal (not fillers)
  • Protected with sealant or ceramic
  • Aftercare guidance to avoid re-swirl

Our process — step by step

  1. Assessment & lighting check — identify defects and measure paint thickness.
  2. Safe wash & decontamination — two-bucket wash, iron fallout & tar removal.
  3. Test section — select the safest combo of pad & polish for your paint type.
  4. Correction stage(s) — single-stage or multi-stage machine polishing.
  5. Finishing & inspection — refine for clarity and consistent gloss.
  6. Protection — sealant or ceramic coating to lock in results.

Aftercare — keep that finish pristine

  • Two-bucket safe wash with grit guards and quality mitts.
  • Use pH-neutral shampoo; avoid harsh roadside chemicals.
  • Dry with clean microfibres; avoid circular motions.
  • Top up protection with spray sealant every few months (for coated cars, use the recommended topper).

We’ll provide a simple aftercare guide tailored to your vehicle and protection choice.

Quick FAQs

Is machine polishing safe?

Yes — in trained hands with paint depth checks and the right pads & polishes. We work within safe limits for your clear coat.

Do scratches come back?

Removed defects don’t reappear, but new ones can be introduced by poor washing. Follow our aftercare to prevent that.

Coating or wax?

Coatings last years and are more resistant. Waxes look great but are short-term. We’ll recommend the best fit for your use and budget.
