About Crystal Coat Detailing

Founded by lifelong enthusiast Daniel Fraser, Crystal Coat Detailing delivers meticulous car care, from daily commuter refreshes to multi-layer ceramic coating installations.

Our Philosophy

Detailing is about respect for every vehicle. We invest the time to understand your paintwork, driving habits, and expectations so we can treat your pride and joy like our own.

Our Approach

We combine industry-leading tools with proven techniques: safe wash systems, calibrated lighting, and ceramic-certified products to guarantee consistent results.

What sets us apart

  • Accredited ceramic coating installer with warranty-backed products.
  • Fully insured mobile or studio appointments across the North East.
  • Tailored maintenance plans to keep finishes pristine all year.
