Owner FAQs

Straight answers to the most common questions from caravan and motorhome owners.

Is the roof included?

Roof cleaning is optional. We recommend adding it as streaks often start on the roof. Toggle it on in the quote form.

How do you access the roof safely?

We use stabilised platforms and ladder standoffs with padding. We never walk unsupported on fragile roofs.

Are your products safe for gel-coat and acrylic windows?

Yes. All chemicals and machines are gel-coat safe, and acrylic windows receive plastics-safe polish and protection only.

Do you need power or water on-site?

Power and water access help, but we can bring solutions. Let us know in the form so we can plan accordingly.

Can you work at storage yards or campsites?

Yes, as long as we have permission. Provide contact details or gate info in the notes and we‚Äôll coordinate.

What about winterising or coatings in rain?

We avoid applying protection in wet or freezing conditions. For ceramics we schedule a dry window or erect cover to control the environment.
