What's included

Our detailing process refreshes every surface. Exterior paintwork is snow-foamed, contact washed, decontaminated, and sealed. Inside we tackle carpets, fabrics, leather, and trims with steaming, extraction, and protection tailored to each material.

  • Exterior: Snow foam pre-wash, two-bucket contact wash, wheel and tyre deep clean, iron and tar removal when required, plus gloss protection.
  • Interior: Full vacuum including crevices, steam cleaning, shampoo extraction for carpets and mats, leather cleansing and conditioning, plastics dressed to a factory finish.
  • Finishing touches: Crystal-clear glass inside and out, odour neutralisation, and optional add-ons such as fabric guard, pet hair removal, or ozone treatment.
