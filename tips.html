Expert Advice — aftercare & maintenance

Keep your finish looking sharp with simple, proven routines. This is the guidance we give customers after correction, ceramic coating or PPF installs.

Maintenance wash (safe)

  • Rinse first to remove loose grit.
  • Pre-wash (snow foam) — let dwell, don’t let it dry.
  • Two-bucket wash with soft mitt; work top to bottom.
  • Separate tools for wheels/arches.

Frequency: Weekly/fortnightly depending on mileage and weather.

Drying safely

  • Sheet water off with a hose; then pat dry with plush towels.
  • Use a drying aid or quick detailer for slickness.
  • Leaf blower / pet dryer helps avoid touching high-risk areas.

Avoid: Beach towels, old T-shirts, chamois — they mar the finish.

Decontamination schedule

  • Iron remover: every 2–3 months or as needed.
  • Tar remover: when you see specks on sills/rear quarters.
  • Clay: only when necessary — always re-protect afterwards.

On coated cars, decon helps restore beading when the surface becomes clogged.

Ceramic coating & PPF care

Ceramic coatings

  • First 7 days: avoid harsh washes or chemicals.
  • Use pH-neutral shampoos; avoid strong APCs.
  • Top up with silica-based spray every few months if desired.

PPF

  • Hand wash with soft mitts; avoid aggressive solvents.
  • Bug splatter & bird droppings: soften with warm, soapy water before wiping.
  • Light swirls may self-heal with warmth/sun.

Winter tips

  • Rinse more often to remove salt.
  • Use pre-wash liberally; keep mitts/towels clean.
  • Consider a decon mid-winter to keep coatings lively.

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Our packages range 1–5+ years. Real-world life depends on wash method, mileage and storage. We include a single-stage machine polish in every coating package for proper bonding.

Do I still need to wash a coated car?

Yes — coatings don’t stop dirt landing; they make it easier and safer to remove. Follow the routine above for swirl-free results.

Will PPF yellow?

Modern quality films are highly resistant. Keep them clean, avoid harsh chemicals, and they’ll stay clear for years.
