Your car is constantly bombarded with contaminants that bond to the paint and cannot be removed by washing alone. The two main types are:

  • Iron Fallout: These are tiny metal particles from brake dust and industrial sources. They embed in your paint and can cause rust spots. Our fallout removers contain a chemical that reacts with these particles, turning purple and safely dissolving them so they can be rinsed away.
  • Tar & Glue: These are sticky deposits from the road surface that cling to the lower parts of your car.

Without removing these bonded contaminants, the paint surface will never be truly clean, and any wax, sealant, or coating you apply won't bond properly or last as long. It’s a critical step for achieving a smooth, durable finish.

A ceramic coating is the ultimate in paint protection, offering a significant upgrade over traditional waxes.

A wax, like the durable hand wax we apply in our Silver Package, is an organic or synthetic layer that sits on top of the paint. It provides great gloss and protection, typically for up to 6 months.

A ceramic coating (containing SiO2, or silica dioxide) is a liquid polymer that forms a semi-permanent, chemical bond with your car's paint. This creates a hard, glassy layer of protection.
