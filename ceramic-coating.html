Preparation makes the difference

Ceramic coatings work best on perfectly prepared surfaces. We inspect every vehicle, measure paint depth where required, and perform the right level of machine polishing before installing a coating matched to your driving style.

  • Thorough wash & decon: Citrus pre-wash, snow foam, two bucket wash, wheel and barrel cleanse, iron & tar removal, plus mechanical clay bar.
  • Machine refinement: Single or multi-stage polish to correct haze and micro-marring so the coating can bond flawlessly.
  • Coating installation: Temperature-controlled curing, panel-by-panel application, and cross-link checks for a uniform, high-gloss finish.
